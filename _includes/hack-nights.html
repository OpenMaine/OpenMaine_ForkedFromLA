Events

We host monthly Hack Nights in downtown Portland, usually on the third Thursday of the month. At these events, we organize groups of volunteers to build technology addressing Maine's biggest civic issues. Each night offers an inclusive, diverse community, and everyone is welcome. Join an existing project team or pitch your own civic issue.
